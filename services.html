Our Services

Comprehensive floor coating solutions for industrial, commercial, and residential applications across Connecticut.

Epoxy Floor Coatings

Our epoxy floor coating systems deliver exceptional durability, chemical resistance, and visual appeal. Whether you need a high-gloss finish for a showroom or a heavy-duty system for a manufacturing plant, we have the right solution.

We install a full range of epoxy systems using Dur-A-Flex products, including solid color, decorative chip/flake, metallic, and quartz broadcast systems.

  • High-build solid color epoxy systems
  • Decorative chip and flake broadcast floors
  • Metallic and marble epoxy finishes
  • Anti-slip and safety striping options
  • Custom color matching available

Urethane Floor Systems

Urethane floor coatings provide superior chemical and thermal resistance, making them ideal for food and beverage facilities, pharmaceutical plants, and commercial kitchens. These systems can withstand hot water wash-downs, steam cleaning, and harsh chemicals.

Our urethane cement systems, including Dur-A-Flex Poly-Crete, create seamless, sanitary floors that meet FDA and USDA requirements.

  • FDA/USDA compliant flooring systems
  • Thermal shock resistant up to 250°F
  • Ideal for breweries and food processing
  • Seamless, easy-to-clean surface
  • Integral cove base available

Concrete Polishing & Grinding

Diamond-polished concrete creates a stunning, mirror-like surface that's durable, low-maintenance, and environmentally friendly. Our multi-step polishing process uses progressively finer diamond abrasives to achieve the desired level of sheen.

Polished concrete is perfect for retail, showrooms, museums, and residential spaces where aesthetics and durability are equally important.

  • Multiple sheen levels available (matte to high-gloss)
  • Stain guard and densifier treatments
  • Exposed aggregate and decorative options
  • Low maintenance and long-lasting
  • Environmentally sustainable solution

Parking Garage Coatings

Our traffic-bearing membrane systems protect parking structure concrete from water infiltration, road salt, chemical spills, and tire abrasion. These elastomeric coatings bridge hairline cracks and provide long-term waterproofing protection.

We install complete parking garage systems including traffic coatings, safety striping, directional markings, and stair tower coatings.

  • Waterproof traffic-bearing membranes
  • Crack-bridging elastomeric systems
  • Safety striping and directional markings
  • Stair tower and ramp coatings
  • Salt and chemical resistance

Surface Preparation

Proper surface preparation is the foundation of every successful floor coating installation. We use industrial-grade equipment to achieve the ideal concrete surface profile (CSP) required for maximum coating adhesion and longevity.

Our preparation methods include shot blasting, scarifying, diamond grinding, and acid etching — selected based on substrate condition and coating requirements.

  • Shot blasting for large-scale prep
  • Scarifying for heavy coatings removal
  • Diamond grinding for precision profiling
  • Moisture and adhesion testing
  • Old coating removal and remediation

Concrete Repair & Maintenance

Before any coating is applied, concrete defects must be properly addressed. We provide comprehensive concrete repair services including joint filling, crack repair, spall patching, and surface restoration.

We also offer maintenance and recoating services for existing floor systems, extending the life of your investment and keeping your facility looking its best.

  • Expansion and control joint filling
  • Crack repair and injection
  • Spall and divot patching
  • Existing coating removal
  • Recoating and maintenance programs
