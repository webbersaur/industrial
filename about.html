About Us

Over 25 years of professional floor coating experience, serving Connecticut and the tri-state area.

Our Story

Industrial Flooring Technologies was founded by Brian Steinle, a hands-on flooring professional with over 25 years of experience installing high-performance floor coating systems. Based in Hamden, Connecticut, the company has built a reputation for quality workmanship, reliability, and attention to detail.

As an independent contractor, Brian works directly with every client — from initial consultation through project completion. This direct involvement ensures consistent quality and clear communication on every project, whether it's a small residential garage or a multi-story industrial facility.

Our crew has installed epoxy, urethane, and polished concrete systems in warehouses, manufacturing plants, breweries, veterinary facilities, fire stations, retail spaces, parking structures, and homes throughout Connecticut and the surrounding region.
